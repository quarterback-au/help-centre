## Engagement Metrics & Content Classification

Better data about how content performs, and smarter classification of what it is.

### What's New

- **Paid vs organic tracking** – Classify activities as paid or organic to understand which content drives genuine engagement versus sponsored reach
- **Platform-specific metrics** – Reddit upvotes and comment counts, YouTube views and watch time, Twitter impressions and retweets now captured per-activity
- **Media alerts** – Media coverage now included in daily and weekly recap emails alongside chatter and announcements
- **Publisher icons** – Brandfetch integration provides recognisable publisher logos throughout the platform

### Improvements

- Board report loading states improved with skeleton placeholders
- Scraper sanitisation for cleaner content display
- Facebook follower tracking added to social metrics

### Bug Fixes

- Fixed search result ordering for recent activities
