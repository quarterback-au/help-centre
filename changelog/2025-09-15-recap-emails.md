## Daily & Weekly Recap Emails

Stay on top of activity without logging in. Quarterback now sends automated recap emails summarising what happened around your company.

### What's New

- **Daily recaps** – A morning summary of the previous day's activities, announcements, and notable sentiment shifts
- **Weekly recaps** – End-of-week overview covering all activity, top authors, and media coverage
- **Price-sensitive filtering** – Filter announcements by price-sensitivity to focus on material disclosures
- **Persistent date ranges** – Selected date ranges now persist across page navigation and sessions

### Bug Fixes

- Fixed forum scraper handling of deleted posts
- Improved database query performance for large activity volumes
