## Dashboard & Share Price Analytics

We've improved the core dashboard experience with better share price tracking and at-a-glance metrics.

### What's New

- **Intraday price updates** – Share prices now refresh throughout the trading day with accurate formatting for ASX-listed securities
- **Stats bar** – Key metrics displayed prominently at the top of the dashboard for quick reference
- **Historical price charts** – Configurable date ranges with consistent formatting across all views
- **Performance improvements** – Faster dashboard loading with optimised data fetching
