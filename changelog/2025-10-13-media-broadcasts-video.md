## Media Page, Broadcasts & Video Tracking

Three new dedicated pages and two new platform integrations. This is our biggest update yet.

### Media Page

A dedicated page for media coverage with publisher rankings, article grids, and filtering by publication, sentiment, and date range. See at a glance which outlets cover your company and how coverage trends over time.

### Broadcast Page

A new home for broadcast content—investor presentations, webinars, and video updates—tracked and displayed separately from chatter and media.

### Video & Social Tracking

- **YouTube integration** – Channel and video scraping with view counts, engagement metrics, and comment tracking
- **Facebook monitoring** – Page-level activity tracking including post engagement and follower counts
- **Announcement side panel** – Preview any announcement in a side panel without leaving the list view

### Improvements

- Engagement metrics now displayed directly on activity detail cards
- Author statistics table with average price change per author
- Mailchimp campaign data available in report views
