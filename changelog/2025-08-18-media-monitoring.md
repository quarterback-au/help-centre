## Media Monitoring

We've partnered with Newscatcher to bring publisher-level media tracking to the platform. You can now see which publications are covering your company, how often, and with what sentiment.

### What's New

- **Publisher metadata** – Every media article now includes publication name, author, and reach metrics from Newscatcher
- **Publisher ranking** – See which publications cover your company most frequently
- **Board report redesign** – Improved layout with better data presentation and clearer section structure
- **Alerts system** – Rebuilt alerts architecture supporting configurable thresholds and multiple delivery channels

### Improvements

- Activity editing now supports inline updates without page reload
- Abnormal returns charting added to announcement detail views
