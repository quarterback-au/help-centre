## Year-End Refinements

A quality-focused update to close out the year. Sentiment analysis is more accurate, alerts are more reliable, and several long-standing issues have been resolved.

### Improvements

- **Sentiment accuracy** – Improved sentiment analysis with better handling of IR-specific language and context
- **Symbol change tracking** – Corporate actions that change ticker symbols are now tracked and handled automatically
- **Alert emails** – Redesigned email templates with cleaner layout and better mobile rendering

### Bug Fixes

- Fixed timezone handling in daily and weekly recap emails
- Fixed AI Insights panel closing unexpectedly during navigation
- Fixed Twitter/X link handling for x.com URLs
- Fixed source icons not displaying correctly for some publishers
- Fixed price change display for single-day activity windows
