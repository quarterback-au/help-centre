## Mailchimp Integration

Connect your Mailchimp account to track email campaign performance alongside all your other investor communications.

### What's New

- **Campaign tracking** – Mailchimp campaigns appear automatically as activities with open rates, click rates, and delivery metrics
- **Performance summaries** – Campaign data displayed directly on activity detail cards
- **Manual activity form** – Redesigned for faster entry with cleaner field layout and better metadata support

### Improvements

- Share price formatting updated for consistency across all displays
- Sidebar navigation cleanup with better grouping of related pages
