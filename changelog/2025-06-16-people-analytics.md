## People Analytics

Understanding who is talking about your company matters as much as what they're saying. We've added people tracking across all monitored platforms.

### What's New

- **Author profiles** – Track individuals across HotCopper, Reddit, Twitter/X, and media publications
- **Engagement history** – See how frequently someone posts about your company and how their sentiment trends over time
- **Cross-platform attribution** – The same person posting on multiple platforms is linked into a single profile where possible
- **Reach indicators** – Understand the relative influence of each person based on their platform presence
