# How to Connect Social Accounts

Link your company's social media profiles to Quarterback to track mentions, engagement, and sentiment across platforms.

## Steps

1. Go to **Settings** > **Social Accounts**
2. Click **Connect Account**
3. Choose the platform you want to connect (e.g. LinkedIn, X/Twitter)
4. Authorise Quarterback to access the account when prompted
5. Once connected, the account will appear in your Social Accounts list

## Supported Platforms

| Platform | What's Tracked |
|----------|---------------|
| LinkedIn | Posts, engagement, follower growth |
| X/Twitter | Tweets, mentions, retweets, sentiment |

## Tips

- You can connect multiple accounts per platform
- Disconnecting an account does not delete historical data
- Data syncing begins within a few minutes of connecting
