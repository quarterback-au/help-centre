# How to Track a Competitor

Add peer companies to your Quarterback workspace to compare their performance, sentiment, and announcements with yours.

## Steps

1. Go to **Settings** > **Market Settings**
2. Scroll to the **Peer Companies** section
3. Click **Add Peer**
4. Search for the company by name or ASX ticker code
5. Select the company from the results
6. Click **Save Changes**

Once added, peer company data will appear in:
- **Dashboard** — Peer comparison widgets
- **Benchmarking** — Side-by-side performance metrics
- **Reports** — Peer sections in generated reports

## Tips

- You can add up to 10 peer companies
- Peer data may take a few hours to fully populate after adding
- Remove peers at any time from the Market Settings page
