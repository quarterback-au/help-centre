# How to Monitor ASX Announcements

Quarterback automatically tracks ASX announcements for your company and peer companies, providing AI-powered sentiment and impact analysis.

## Viewing Announcements

1. Navigate to **Announcements** from the main sidebar
2. Browse the list of recent announcements
3. Use filters to narrow results:
   - **Company** — Your company or specific peers
   - **Type** — Earnings, capital raise, trading halt, etc.
   - **Date range** — Custom or preset periods

## Understanding the Analysis

Each announcement includes:
- **Sentiment score** — AI-assessed tone of the announcement
- **Market impact** — Share price movement in the hours following release
- **Abnormal return** — How the price moved compared to expected returns
- **Media pickup** — Whether the announcement was covered by news outlets

## Setting Up Announcement Alerts

To be notified of new announcements:
1. Go to **Alerts** > **Create Alert**
2. Select **New Announcement** as the trigger
3. Choose which companies to monitor
4. Save the alert
