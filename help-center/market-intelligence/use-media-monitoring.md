# How to Use Media Monitoring

Stay on top of news coverage about your company with Quarterback's media monitoring tools.

## Viewing Media Coverage

1. Navigate to **Media Monitoring** from the main sidebar
2. Browse articles sorted by recency
3. Each article shows:
   - **Headline and source**
   - **Sentiment score** — AI-assessed tone
   - **Reach estimate** — Potential audience size
   - **Publication date**

## Filtering Coverage

Use the filters to find specific articles:
- **Source type** — News, blog, industry publication
- **Sentiment** — Positive, neutral, or negative
- **Date range** — Custom or preset periods
- **Keywords** — Search within article text

## Tips

- Click any article to read a summary and view the full source
- Negative coverage is flagged with a red indicator for quick identification
- Media coverage is included in automated reports if the Media section is enabled
