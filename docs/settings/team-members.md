# Team Members

Multiple people can access Quarterback to monitor activities, review announcements, and generate reports.

---

## Managing Access

Team management is handled through Outseta (the billing and account platform).

To add or remove team members, contact [support@qback.au](mailto:support@qback.au).

:::info
All team members share the same organisation data — activities, announcements, reports, and settings. Individual preferences like flagged authors and archived activities are scoped per user.
:::
