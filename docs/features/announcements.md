# Announcements

The announcements page is purpose-built for tracking ASX announcements and their market impact.

## Grid View

Announcements display in an AG Grid with powerful filtering and sorting:

### Columns

- Date and time
- Title (with price-sensitive badge)
- Close price, Change %, Volume
- CAR (Cumulative Abnormal Return) over 10 trading days
- Average sentiment from linked activities
- Linked activity count

**Default Filter:** Price-sensitive announcements only. Toggle to show all announcements.

## Announcement Details

Click any announcement to open the detail panel:

### Market Section

- Mini candlestick showing OHLC for the day
- Daily return with trending indicator
- Performance vs index (outperformance metric)
- Event Study CAR Chart (10 days before and after)

### Activity Categories

Activities linked to the announcement are categorized:

- **Own Distribution** – Your broadcasts via LinkedIn, Twitter, Mailchimp
- **Media Pickup** – News coverage with source breakdown
- **Paid Distribution** – Sponsored placements
- **Community Discussion** – Social forum activity

### Sentiment Analysis

Visual sentiment distribution showing positive, neutral, and negative breakdown with average score.

## Linking Activities

Connect activities to announcements to build context around market reaction:

1. Open the announcement detail panel
2. Click "Link Activities"
3. Search and select activities from other days
4. Activities appear in the linked section

Linked activities contribute to the announcement's sentiment average and appear in reports.

## Export Reports

Generate HTML reports for board packs or compliance records:

1. Select announcements using checkboxes (or select all)
2. Click Export in the toolbar
3. Choose announcements to include
4. Report opens in new tab—print or save as PDF

Reports include market metrics, activity breakdown, sentiment analysis, and linked activity details.
