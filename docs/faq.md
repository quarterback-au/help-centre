# FAQs

## How often is data updated?

Activities are collected continuously. Share prices update during market hours. Commodities update hourly.

## Can I export data?

Yes. Most grids support Excel export. Reports can be saved as PDF via browser print.

## How is sentiment calculated?

Machine learning models analyze text content and assign scores from -1 (negative) to +1 (positive).

## What's the difference between AR and CAR?

AR (Abnormal Return) is single-day performance vs expected. CAR (Cumulative Abnormal Return) sums multiple days.

## Can I track multiple companies?

Each organization can monitor one primary entity. Contact support for multi-entity requirements.

## How do I get help?

Click Support in the profile menu or email support@qback.au.
